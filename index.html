Who We Are

Built on 17 years of field presence.

Development, Culture & Leadership is a Lebanese NGO headquartered in Syr Al Dennieh, North Lebanon, registered and operating since 2008. We design and implement accountable humanitarian, education, and food security programmes across Lebanon in partnership with institutional donors, UN agencies, ministries, and local municipalities.

Mission

To deliver accountable, community-centred programmes that improve access to education, food security, and social protection for vulnerable populations across Lebanon.

Approach

Field-first. Every programme is built around operational presence, community trust, and monitoring systems that ensure quality from first mile to last. We measure what matters.

Accountability

Independent monitoring, transparent donor reporting, PSEA compliance, and community feedback channels are embedded into every programme we run, not added as an afterthought. Accountability runs from the first mile to the last.

What drives us

Our vision & values.

Our Vision

A society where people from all walks of life, faiths, and communities come together in respect and collaboration.

By promoting Peace, Love, and Reconciliation, we aim to create inclusive spaces where dialogue replaces division, empathy replaces mistrust, and shared responsibility strengthens social bonds. Our goal is to inspire and model these interactions regionally, so diversity becomes a source of strength rather than conflict.

We pursue this vision through:
  • Education & Nutrition
  • Food Security
  • Youth Development
  • Emergency Response

Accountability mechanism

Feedback & Complaints

DCL is committed to accountability to the communities we serve. All submissions are treated confidentially and responded to within 5 working days.

All submissions are handled by DCL's accountability focal point and kept strictly confidential. You are not required to share your name.

Misconduct Report

Submit a Report

Your report goes directly to DCL's accountability focal point — not to general management. You may remain anonymous.

Your name and email will not be included

By submitting you confirm this is a genuine concern. DCL will investigate and respond within 5 working days where contact details are provided.

Beneficiary Feedback

Share Your Feedback

Your voice helps us improve. Share your experience with any DCL programme — positive or negative. You may remain anonymous.

Your name and contact will not be included

Your feedback is reviewed by DCL's accountability team. If you left contact details, we will follow up within 5 working days.

Direct Email

Email Us Directly

Send your complaint or concern directly to complaints@dclngo.org. Read the guidance below first.

What to include

Describe what happened, when, and where. Include the programme or location if relevant. You do not need to provide evidence — just your account of events.

Staying anonymous

If you wish to remain anonymous, do not include your name, phone number, or personal email in the message body. Consider using a temporary email address (e.g. a new Gmail or ProtonMail account) so your identity is not revealed by your sender address.

Confidentiality

All emails to complaints@dclngo.org are received only by DCL's designated accountability focal point. Your message will not be shared with general staff or the subject of any complaint.

Response time

DCL will acknowledge your message within 2 working days and provide a full response within 5 working days, where contact details have been provided.
